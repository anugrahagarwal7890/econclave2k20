Our flagship event, E-Conclave, has always been at the pinnacle in cultivating Entrepreneurial culture. It is the only cluster of innovative ideas and is becoming a boon for the upcoming generation to show their unique personality and ideas.

Lt. Gen. V. K. Chaturvedi is an alumnus of National Defence Academy and Indian Military Academy. He was commissioned into the Regiment of Artillery on 13 June 1971. Immediately on commissioning and after undergoing a curtailed Young Officers Course, he was posted to 99 Field Regiment in the Eastern Sector. He fought the 1971 Indo-Pak war and was awarded the Sena Medal for gallantry.
The officer was awarded the Param Vishisht Seva Medal (PVSM) in 2011 for distinguished services of the highest order by the President of India, the Ati Vishisht Seva Medal (AVSM) for distinguished service of exceptional order by the President of India in 2007.
Lt.General Chaturvedi takes a keen interest in personal and professional issues concerning military personnel. He handled the 6CPC and has been responsible for initiating a number of initiatives for the welfare of all ranks.

Lt. Gen. V.K.Chaturvedi

E-conclave 2019

E-Conclave 2019 was the annual flagship event of E-Cell, GLA University effectuated with the intention to foster entrepreneurial initiatives and activities in the campus and region. It would bring students, new-age entrepreneurs, enthusiasts and venture capitalists to a common ground to engage in plenary, strategic and tactical breakout sessions with the common aim of promoting the entrepreneurship spirit.

E Conclave 2019 was based on the theme Step up to Startup: India’s Entrepreneurial Revolution was a nationwide event i.e. open to students from across the nation. With the Business Plan Competition being the centre of attraction for, our aim is to encourage maximum participation from our region and have quality entrepreneurial ideas coming in. The footfall of the event would easily be over 1500 and the outreach of the event could be expected around thousands of more young and enthusiastic audience. This would allow us to successfully preach the entrepreneurship, as an institution and concept.

The footstep of the journey started with the orientation of the ceremony of E-cell 4.0. The main aim behind the orientation ceremony was to introduce the students with the E-cell and its activities, its main motive and many more.
All the council members, the executive was in the planning mood to execute the one of the most important events of E-Conclave Movie Screening which aims to energise the individuals in order to inculcate the entrepreneurial spirit among them.

After successfully executing the Movie Screening event, the team was in the full enthusiasm to decide its next mega event, in the context of the same and it was decided that 50 Rs Venture will be in limelight for upcoming days.
In the present era of Startup revolution, E-cell was also touched with it. In the context of the same, it introduced its next event named ‘Mergers and Acquisitions’, the idea behind this was to test the start-up skills of the young minds.

The next thriller was about the Panel Discussion and grand opening ceremony of E-Conclave 2019. Opening witnessed a crowd of about 700-800 students. The Panel Discussion witnessed some of the top speakers of the country giving their valuable talk in the discussion.

E-Conclave goes for the hunt in the form of ‘Entrepreneurial Jeopardy’ among the youth. The event was a great success as it was a boon for the students to showcase their talent to everyone. The event was organized in the 4 rounds consisting the reward of 6000 rupees.

The next event in E-Conclave was B-Plan Competition. Innovation and entrepreneurship are the two sides of the same coin that steer the world into a brighter future. The world economy will shatter upon itself if new innovations are not reaped every day.

B-plan provides a platform for potential, great and million-dollar ideas at an early-stage start-up to evaluate their plan in the risk-free environment of a competition. It aims to bridge the gap between ideas and the reality of the industry. B-plan is one of the foremost events for budding entrepreneurs: a place providing entrepreneurs with the best possible resources – including education, networking, and new generation – to help turn fruitful and innovative ideas into reality.

Technology in the current 21st Century has become an integral part of every individual. Keeping this in mind the next event of E-Conclave was ‘BlockChain Workshop’ which was very well executed and students also got to learn about the key aspect of it.

A thriller should be a part of everyone’s life. In E-Conclave also, keeping the same view we organized ‘Rocketry Workshop which told students about the key aspect of the rocket and at the end a demonstration of the rocket launch was also given by the team. Writers and Writing goes hand in hand.E-Conclave also witnessed the ‘Melting With Words’ workshop based on content writing. The event was a great success and it witnessed around 100-150 students.

The mega event which was based on the theme i.e Step up to Startup.E-Conclave also tries to be true with it by organising a workshop on ‘How to start a Startup.’ The spokesperson told all the things related to it and all the buddy students were very fascinated about it.

After all these, the closing ceremony was organized with the view of making all the tension, the pressure goes out of their mind by having an event called TRANCE-MEE.
